An application that instantly tells you if products in the grocery store are safe for people with allergies. Just scan the bar-code and know the risk for you and your loved ones!

Three tiers available:

Free - Scan a barcode and get a list of safe / not safe for the major allergenes.
Month to month subscription - Create your allergene profile online and get an instant answer if the product is safe / not safe for you. Up to two allergene profiles.
Annual subscriptions - Create allergene profile online for you and your family (number of profiles dependant on subscription) and get instant answers if the product is safe for each family member.
